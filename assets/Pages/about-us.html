About Us

We,the sincere youth of our progressive nation,have started an initiative of educating India and Nepal’s those students, who has made up their minds to quit their education just because of the skyhigh fee of higher educations. Our only motive is to make these expensive Higher educations so cheaper to the common man, that it can be made available to all the students.

“ Campus Sarthi ” is the name of such an initiative which helped thousands of students to take admission in their favourable courses and colleges at 100% scholarship. Every year “Campus Sarthi” adopts approximately 500 students who takes the benefits of our Scholarship “Sarthi” by getting the education on the contrary of paying fooding and lodging amount.

“ Campus Sarthi ” is a rapidly rising admission cell of India and Nepal which is being run for the interest of students and parents through “Sarthi Educational and Charitable Trust”.

What do Campus Sarthi wants from you ?

Our “Sarthi”team is doing best on our level to find the brilliant and aimful students from each and every rural areas of the country,but we need a huge team for this purpose. We must have associates from every district,who will help the students to connect with our scholarship program “Sarthi”. It will be very pleasure for us if you look after your district’s work assignments.We want only 50 students from your district who has got 50% marks in their secondary and higher secondary education and wants to get helped for their further studies. You have to Make them available our scholarship of “Campus Sarthi” .

For further details please contact our expert team on 9229011108 or visit our official website www.campussarthi.com

Campus Sarthi believes in the transformative power of education by our dedications and full determinations to make an easy way for the aspiring students to achieve their dreams.
